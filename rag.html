RAG Pipeline

Step 1

Document Ingestion

The system receives knowledge documents.

Step 2

Chunking

Large text is split into smaller chunks.

Step 3

Embedding

Each chunk is converted into a searchable meaning vector.

Step 4

Retrieval

The system finds the chunks most relevant to the question.

Step 5

Prompt Building

The question and retrieved context are combined.

Step 6

Answer Generation

The AI generates an answer using the retrieved context.
